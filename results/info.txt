When using mat1, the closest days are generated by only considering Dew Point and Temperature.
When using mat2, the closest days are generated by only considering Global Horizontal Irradiance, Cloud Cover Percentage, and Direct Horizontal Irradiance.
When using mat3, the closest days are generated by only considering WindSpeed at surface level and WindSpeed at 100mph.

results_matX are .csv files that contain the 10 closest days for every day starting from 1940 to 2023 as generated by 
the algorithm for the respective properties that are compared. These results are based on weather data in Texas only.
